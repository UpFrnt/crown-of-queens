About Us

We opened Crown of Queens in 2020 because we noticed a need for more access to Black beauty products in our area. We offer products that support healthy hair and skin - including shampoos, oils, soaps, hair bundles, wigs, and more. Our online store also offers an array of fashionable items and accessories. We know your beauty is important, and we think you should be able to get the look you want regardless of your budget.

Our customer service is predicated on supplying premium quality beauty products at the best possible price. We have been able to sustain our excellent customer relationships by ensuring that we supply precisely what our customers need, and more. Our customer care service is second to none – if you have an issue with any of our products, we’re on hand to help sort out your problems and also recommend more suitable products. We provide bespoke advice for our products, catering to each customer separately and professionally.

Wigs, Bundles, and More!

If you’re looking for something requiring less maintenance, braided hair extensions and wigs may be exactly what you need. With good hair products and maintenance, they can last for months. For a special event, wigs are your best friend - you can easily put them on and take them off yourself. They also provide great volume and are durable so you can use them again and again.

Visit Crown of Queens and check out our selection of extensions and wigs. We also have the best products to help you maintain that fabulous look!
